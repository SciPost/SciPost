Why SciPost?

  • Info for scientists
  • Info for librarians/Open Access officers of universities and funding agencies

TL;DR

There are plenty of reasons to switch to SciPost for your scientific publishing needs. Here are a few.

  • SciPost is by-and-for scientists
  • We offer a fully-featured professional publishing infrastructure
  • All publications at SciPost carry open licenses (CC-BY by default)
  • Authors maintain copyright of their work
  • Our operations are entirely not-for-profit
  • SciPost does not charge any subscription or author fees (APCs)
  • We implement an industry-transforming, cost-slashing business model
  • SciPost has a resolutely international outlook
  • We offer a quality-focused family of Journals
  • Our open refereeing system provides robust and utility-focused pre-publication review
  • Referees get credit for their work through citable reports
  • All editorial decisions at all levels are taken by professionally active scientists
  • Decisions are based exclusively on scientific quality
  • SciPost is entirely free of undue business/profit-making secondary interests
  • Our infrastructure is built on Free(d) and Open Source Software
  • Our production facilities are "top of the class"
  • All publications are professionally archived for long-term posterity
  • SciPost is pushing for modernization of impact evaluation
