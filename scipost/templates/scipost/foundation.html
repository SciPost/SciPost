The SciPost Foundation (Stichting SciPost)

The SciPost Foundation, Inc. is a nonprofit charitable organization dedicated to developing, implementing and maintaining innovative forms of electronic scientific communication and publishing. The SciPost Foundation operates the SciPost.org interactive online portal for the benefit of the international scientific community and further interested parties.

You can consult the Act of Foundation (official version, in Dutch).

You can also consult our most recent business plan.
