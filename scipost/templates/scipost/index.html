About SciPost

SciPost is a complete scientific publication portal managed by active professional scientists.

It is purely online-based and offers freely, openly, globally and perpetually accessible science.

Journals

SciPost publishes a portfolio of high-quality open access, charge-free scientific journals.

All SciPost Journals implement the maximally stringent peer-witnessed refereeing principle.

All Journals are fully managed by professional scientists.
