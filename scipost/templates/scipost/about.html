About SciPost

SciPost is a complete scientific publication portal. It is purely online-based, and offers freely, openly, globally and perpetually accessible science. Being managed by professional scientists, and making use of editor-sollicited and contributed reviews, it aims at the highest achievable standards of refereeing.

Guiding principles

The guiding principles in the conception, implementation and administration of SciPost are:

  • stringent refereeing maximizes the quality of scientific publications
  • publicly-funded science should be openly accessible to scientists and the general public, perpetually, worldwide
  • peer evaluation is not completed at the moment of publication, but is an ongoing process

The first principle is implemented using SciPost's maximally stringent peer-witnessed refereeing process.

The second principle is reflected in SciPost's not-for-profit status, financially backed by the generous support of national granting agencies, universities, foundations and individuals (see Acknowledgements below).

The third principle is implemented in the form of Commentary Pages associated to publications. These can either be SciPost publications, or papers published elsewhere.
