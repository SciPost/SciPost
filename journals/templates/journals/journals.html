SciPost Physics

SciPost Physics publishes outstanding-quality research articles in all domains and specializations of Physics.

The journal accepts two types of content: Letters and Articles.

Letters report broad-interest, significant breakthroughs in Physics, of interest and importance to researchers in multiple specializations.

Articles provide in-depth, detailed reports of groundbreaking research within one or more specializations.

SciPost Physics Select

SciPost Physics Select publishes articles of superlative quality in the field of Physics.

Authors cannot submit directly to this Journal; SPS papers are editorially selected from the most outstanding Submissions to SciPost Physics.
