SciPost Physics is a multifaceted journal publishing high-quality research in the domains of Experimental, Theoretical and Computational physics. The Journal publishes papers in the following specialties:

  • A: Atomic, Molecular and Optical Physics
  • B: Biophysics
  • C: Condensed Matter Physics
  • F: Fluid Dynamics
  • G: Gravitation, Cosmology and Astroparticle Physics
  • H: High-Energy Physics
  • M: Mathematical Physics
  • N: Nuclear Physics
  • Q: Quantum Statistical Mechanics
  • S: Statistical and Soft Matter Physics
