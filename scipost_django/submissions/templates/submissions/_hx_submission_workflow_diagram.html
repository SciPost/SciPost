Legend
  • ✉=email sent
  • S=SciPost EdAdmin
  • A=submitting Author
  • F=Fellow(s)
  • E=Editor-in-charge
  • R=Referee(s)
  • C=Contributor/commenter

Examples:

  • ✉A signifies email from SciPost to submitting author
  • ✉E,R?C? signifies emails to Editor-in-charge, Referee (if ∃), Commenter (if ∃)
