SciPost and the Impact Factor

  • It greatly disfavours emerging publishing venues
  • Any feature it reflects is already outdated, at least for emerging Journals.

Do not be misled by incorrect information from Google

A simple search for "SciPost Physics impact factor" (as of 2020-08-01) on Google brings about a "featured snippet" with journal details stating that the impact factor is 1.067. This is false information. We have contacted Google about this, but have not received any acknowledgement from them, let alone a proposal for a solution.

This false information from Google is of course very damaging to our initiative, since most people will use this search engine and not think twice about whether the info is correct or not.
